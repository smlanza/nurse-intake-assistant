Nurse Intake Assistant - Product Vision and Future Enhancements

Vision

Create an intelligent nurse intake platform that automates patient communication, documentation, triage support, and administrative assistance while maintaining human oversight.

⸻

Phase 2 Features

Patient Identity Verification

The system shall compare patient information against a patient database.

Possible verification fields:

* Name
* Date of birth
* Phone number

⸻

Administrative Self-Service

The system shall answer common patient questions such as:

* Office hours
* Clinic location
* Appointment confirmation
* Clinic policies

⸻

Enhanced Urgency Detection

The system shall classify requests as:

* Routine
* Urgent
* Emergency

Emergency situations shall instruct the patient to seek immediate assistance.

⸻

Nurse Workflow Management

The system shall support:

* New
* In Review
* Completed

case statuses.

⸻

Escalation Workflow

If a nurse does not acknowledge a case within a configurable period:

* Notify backup nurse
* Notify supervisor

⸻

Document Upload Processing

Nurses shall be able to upload:

* Referral documents
* Medical records
* Faxed forms
* PDFs

The system shall extract relevant information using AI.

⸻

Document Intelligence Integration

The system shall:

* Perform OCR
* Extract structured fields
* Generate summaries
* Attach extracted information to cases

⸻

Patient Communication

The system shall support:

* Follow-up SMS messages
* Appointment reminders
* Case status notifications

⸻

Multi-Agent Architecture

The platform may evolve into specialized agents:

Intake Agent

Collects patient information.

Triage Agent

Performs urgency assessment.

Document Agent

Processes uploaded documents.

Notification Agent

Handles SMS and email communication.

⸻

Long-Term Goal

Provide a complete AI-assisted nurse intake platform that reduces administrative workload while maintaining clinical oversight and patient safety.
