Purpose of the Nurse Intake Assistant

Nurse Intake Assistant accepts patient intake text, generates nurse-facing summaries, identifies possible urgency or red-flag indicators, tracks missing intake fields, stores case data, and may send configured demo email or SMS notifications. The application is intended to support review workflows, not to replace clinical judgment.

Information the App May Collect

Depending on configuration and use, the application may collect or generate:

  • Patient-provided intake text.
  • Name, date of birth, callback number, symptoms, reason for calling, and other information typed by the user.
  • Generated summaries, urgency classifications, missing-field indicators, case status, and notification status.
  • Technical metadata such as timestamps, source system IDs, request IDs, logs, diagnostic events, and error details.
  • Hosting and service configuration details associated with [Hosting/Cloud Provider Details].

Information may be used to:

  • Generate intake summaries and structured case records.

  • Test, debug, secure, monitor, evaluate, and improve the application.
  • Investigate reliability, security, misuse, or compliance concerns.

AI Processing Notice

The application may use AI services to summarize intake text and identify possible urgency indicators. AI outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, biased, or unsuitable for a specific clinical situation. AI-generated summaries and urgency indicators are decision-support only and require human review by qualified personnel before any operational or clinical use.
