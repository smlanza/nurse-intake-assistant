Nurse Intake Assistant

Local demo intake assistant using mock-safe backend services.

This demo is for educational use only, is not medical advice, not for emergencies, and AI output requires nurse review before clinical use.

Demo Workflow

  • Step 1Seed, load, and filter the nurse queue.
  • Step 2Review Queue Summary.
  • Step 3Review Recent Cases.
  • Step 4Mark a selected case reviewed.
  • Step 5Submit a text intake.
  • Step 6Submit a voicemail transcript intake.
  • Step 7Reset demo when done.
